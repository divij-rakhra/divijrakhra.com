The Easiest Way To Get Inbound Leads For Free On LinkedIn

April 2026 · 6 min

"Comment 'SWIPE' and I'll send it to you."

I know. It's cringe. You hate these posts. But here's what you can't argue with: they generate massive revenue.

Let's break down the AutoDM funnel that's dominating LinkedIn right now.

The Funnel Structure

Step 1: Create a valuable asset (template, swipe file, framework)

Step 2: Write a LinkedIn post offering the asset in exchange for a comment

Step 3: Send DM with link to landing page

Step 4: Landing page captures email

Step 5: Thank you page with video sales letter + call booking

Step 6: 30-day email nurture sequence

Each step compounds. Miss one, you leave money on the table.

The Asset: Make It Specific

Generic assets fail. "My content templates" won't work.

Specific assets convert. "50 LinkedIn posts for Series A SaaS founders raising their next round."

The more specific, the higher the perceived value. The higher the perceived value, the more comments you get.

The Post: Structure That Converts

The Hook — Aspirational claim with authority: "I've closed $5M worth of inbound revenue from LinkedIn."

The Asset Introduction — "I'm giving away the swipe file of posts that get people flooding my DMs asking to pay me."

Value Enhancement — "Hundreds of high-performing posts for agency owners, staged by objective. Listicles. Case studies. The works."

Scarcity (optional) — Create urgency if relevant.

The CTA — "Comment 'SWIPE' and I'll send it. Must be connected."

The Engagement Strategy

When comments roll in, reply to each one. Fast.

The algorithm rewards engagement velocity. More replies = more reach = more comments.

Don't just send the link. Ask a question: "What's the biggest challenge you're facing with LinkedIn content right now?"

This turns a one-way transaction into a sales conversation.

The Landing Page

Keep it simple. One headline. One form. One CTA.

"Get the LinkedIn Swipe File" → Email input → "Send Me The Templates"

Expect 60-80% conversion rates. These are warm leads who already engaged with you.

The Thank You Page

This is where sales happen.

Include a video sales letter. Show your face. Explain the problem you solve. Why it matters. How you solve it. Social proof.

Add a bold CTA: "Book a free 60-minute strategy session."

This is where cold traffic becomes warm prospects.

The Email Sequence

They gave you their email. Now nurture them.

Send the asset immediately. Then one email per day for 30 days.

Each email: one pain point, one insight, one way you can help.

By day 30, they know you. They trust you. They're ready to buy.

Automating The Process

You don't need to manually DM everyone. Tools exist.

Look into LinkedIn automation platforms. Set up triggers. When someone comments with your keyword, the DM sends automatically.

Your job: Create the asset. Write the post. Engage with genuine conversations. Close deals.

The Math

One post. 2,500 comments. 1,500 email signups. 100 booked calls. 20 closed deals.

Cost: $0.

Time investment: 4 hours for asset creation, 2 hours for the post, 30 minutes daily engagement.

This is the highest-ROI marketing channel available right now. It won't last forever.
