30 Days LinkedIn Content In 30 Minutes

April 2026 · 6 min

Most founders overthink content. They stare at a blank screen. Delete. Rewrite. Publish nothing.

This system creates weeks of LinkedIn content in 30 minutes. Not generic AI slop. Content that sounds like you.

The 5-Step Workflow

Step 1: Ideation

Step 2: Writing

Step 3: Editing

Step 4: Publishing

Step 5: Analysis

Ideation: The Question Bank

The problem isn't running out of ideas. It's not knowing what your audience actually wants to hear about.

Here's the prompt:

"Act as a content marketing expert. Generate 50 highly specific questions my target audience is asking about [industry/topic]. These should be questions they discuss internally with their teams."

Output: 50 questions your dream client is literally asking right now.

Pick one. Move to writing.

Writing: The LinkedIn Structure

Your insights aren't enough. They need to be packaged for how people consume LinkedIn.

The prompt:

"Take these raw notes about [topic] and turn them into a viral LinkedIn post. Use this structure:

- Hook (2 lines before 'see more')

- Social proof

- Dream outcome

- Pain agitation

- The solution/insight

- Call to action

Write like a founder sharing experience, not a blog post. Use short sentences. Add line breaks."

The 10x Better Prompt

Before publishing, run the output through one more prompt:

"Make this 10x more specific, 10x more unique, 10x more actionable. Add concrete numbers. Add proof. Make it impossible to ignore."

This transforms generic content into something worth reading.

Editing: The Non-Negotiable Step

Do not skip this. It's your personal brand. Every post is your reputation.

Read every line. Check for factual errors. Remove fluff. Add personality. Make sure it sounds like YOU.

AI gets you 80% there. The last 20% is your voice.

Publishing: Schedule Natively

Use LinkedIn's native scheduler, not third-party tools.

Studies show 10-15% reach drop when posting through scheduling platforms. LinkedIn's scheduler works fine. You don't need Premium.

Create 30 posts in one session. Schedule them for the next month.

Analysis: Find Your Winners

Metrics that matter:

Not likes. Likes are vanity.

Not comments. Comments can be bots.

Leads and conversations.

Track which posts generate DMs. Which posts lead to calls. Which posts bring revenue.

Use UTM parameters on links. Ask on sales calls: "Which post made you reach out?"

The best clients are lurkers. They never engage. They just watch. Then they book.

The Truth About LinkedIn

Write "how I did X" posts, not "how to do X" posts.

How I did X shows authority. How to do X is just research. Anyone can research. Not everyone has done.

Your experience is your moat. Use it.

What to Do Today

Run the ideation prompt. Get 50 questions.

Pick one. Write your raw thoughts.

Run it through the writing prompt.

Edit manually.

Schedule.

Repeat 30 times.

You now have a month of content. Done in 30 minutes.
